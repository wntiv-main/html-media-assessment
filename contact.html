    Entry

    Contact the eSports Prefect (chs_eports@cashmere.school.nz) or the corresponding teacher to enter the team. Please not that all participating students MUST:

    • Be achieving and up to date with school-based assessments and coursework expectations.
    • Have maintained at least a 90% attendance record for all classes.
    • Agree to represent the school in a positive manner.
    • Have provided written approval to compete from a parent or guardian.
